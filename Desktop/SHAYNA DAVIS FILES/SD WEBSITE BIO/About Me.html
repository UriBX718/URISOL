A Glimpse of Shayna










Born on the 29th of October, 1991 in New York City as the middle child of five Shayna Beatrice Davis has always had her head in the clouds. As a child she always found herself in nature observing all that surrounded her. lways moving from place to place Shayna found a love for travel. As she did she was always inspired by culture, geography, and especially the arts.With these curiosities she would express herself the best way she knew how; throught creative arts such as painting, arts and crafts, poetry, and short stories. Growing into adolescence commencing to truly find herself she found inspiration and guidance through Khemetic Science. Indulging in higher consciousness with the study of metaphysics, alchemy, meditation, and becoming her greatest version. Incorporating her passions and spirituality Shayna Davis is a whimsical, adventurous, edgy yet poised aspiring entreprenuer.
